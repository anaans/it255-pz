About Us


Renta a bike wants to give people a great time by showing them Belgrade in a fun and informative way. We believe a bike-tour is the best way to experience all sides of Belgrade in one go. Although biking is a way of transport which is not used a lot in Belgrade, we developed routes that visit its old parts, its new parts, its nature and its rivers and catch its atmosphere.
